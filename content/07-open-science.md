We are committed to open-science principles and make our early work available as [arXiv pre-prints](https://sensorlab.github.io/publications/), with openly accessible versions of our peer-reviewed papers, [datasets](http://log-a-tec.eu) and [code](https://github.com/sensorlab). Recent open releases include the [Antarctica GNSS dataset](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-025-06366-x), the [multi-region electricity knowledge graph](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-024-04310-z) for data-driven electricity management, and the [indoor radio mapping dataset](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.00494) combining 3D point clouds and RSSI &mdash; all published in *Scientific Data* or as open pre-prints with reproducible pipelines on GitHub.
