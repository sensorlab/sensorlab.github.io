We developed the [LOG-a-TEC](http://log-a-tec.eu) experimental infrastructure for outdoor and indoor experimentation with ultra-narrow-band and ultra-wide-band radios, packet-based experimentation, clean-slate protocol design, composable modular protocol stacks, and advanced spectrum sensing and signal generation in sub-GHz spectrum. Beyond LOG-a-TEC, we run targeted measurement campaigns that have produced new public datasets, including a [comprehensive GNSS dataset from a ship expedition to Antarctica](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-025-06366-x) for studying interference in polar regions, and indoor radio mapping with combined [3D point clouds and RSSI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.00494).
