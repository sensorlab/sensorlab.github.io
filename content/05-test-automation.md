We pioneered continuous integration for embedded development with the [COINS](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/8515680) framework, evaluated on [LOG-a-TEC](http://log-a-tec.eu/index.html), and worked on efficient [zero-touch device provisioning](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9348119). Today we extend that line into the AI-driven orchestration of distributed services across the edge–cloud continuum: a [multi-agent reinforcement-learning in-place scaling engine for edge-cloud systems](https://doi.org/10.1109/CLOUD67622.2025.00014), demonstrated as [smart scaling of AI services for future networks](https://doi.org/10.1109/WCNC61545.2025.10978455), an [analysis of AI techniques for orchestrating edge-cloud application migration](https://doi.org/10.1109/MeditCom64437.2025.11104264), and the [Smart Highway zero-touch service management framework](https://doi.org/10.1109/BalkanCom61808.2024.10557173) for automotive services.
