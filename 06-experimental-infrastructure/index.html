Experimental infrastructure

Published: Monday, January 1, 1

We developed the LOG-a-TEC experimental infrastructre that enables a wide range of outdoor and indoor experimentation such as with ultra narrow band and ultra wide band, packet based experimentation, clean slate protocol design, composable and modular protocol stacks, custom and advanced spectrum sensing and signal generating functions in sub-GHz spectrum.
