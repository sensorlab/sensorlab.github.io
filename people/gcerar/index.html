Dr. Gregor Cerar

  • Role: Postdoc
  • Joined: October, 2016
  • Affiliations: ComSensus, Jožef Stefan Institute
  • Research Interests: Artificial Intelligence, Smart Infrastructure, Wireless Networks, and MLOps.
  • Social: LinkedIn, Scholar, GitHub, Blog, Sicris

Gregor Cerar earned his Bachelor’s degree (2013) and Master’s degree (2016) from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering at the University of Ljubljana, where he completed the Telecommunications study program. He obtained his Ph.D. in Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) in 2021 from the International Postgraduate School of Jožef Stefan, in collaboration with the Department of Communication Systems at the Jožef Stefan Institute.

Currently, he holds a part-time postdoctoral position with the Department of Communication Systems at the Jožef Stefan Institute and works as a research engineer at ComSensus.
