Dr. Blaž Bertalanič

  • Role: Postdoc
  • Joined: October, 2021
  • Affiliations: Jožef Stefan Institute
  • Research Interests: solving classification problems with the help of machine learning and AI, applications of graph netural networks, wireless networking, electronics, and signal processing.
  • Social: LinkedIn, Scholar, GitHub, Sicris, X (Twitter)

Blaž Bertalanič received his Master’s degree in electrical engineering from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, University of Ljubljana. He completed his PhD at the same faculty in 2024 and is now a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Communication Systems, Jožef Stefan Institute.
