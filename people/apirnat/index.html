Anže Pirnat

  • Role: MSc Student
  • Joined: June, 2021
  • Affiliations: Jožef Stefan Institute
  • Research Interests: Artificial Intelligence, Smart Infrastructure, and MLOps.

Anže Pirnat received a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering in 2023 from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, University of Ljubljana. He is pursuing a master’s degree in electrical engineering and working as a research intern at the Department of Communication Systems, Jože Stefan Institute. His work and interests include machine learning, smart infrastructure, and MLOps.
